It’s always #HappyOrderingWithBig6Delivery. “We’re fast! And I do mean really fast!” If you should ever meet our founder, Phillip, that’s a statement you’re most likely going to hear. He’s an annoyingly thoughtful and overly systematic guy, but that’s why we love him (lol), as it led to the creation of Big 6 Delivery (B6 Couriers). This happened after he waited for 3 hours, 26 minutes, and 57 seconds, for another company to deliver lunch on his birthday (he timed it…see what I mean). Big 6 Delivery (B6 Couriers) is a food delivery start-up based at the UWI, Mona campus, in Kingston, Jamaica. It opened on February 15, 2020; five days after his birthday. He considers Big 6 a birthday present. Big 6 Delivery was firstly created for students, staff and residents on and around the UWI, Mona, community. We have since expanded our service areas across Kingston and St. Andrew. Big 6 Delivery initially delivered from these six restaurants located at the UWI, Mona campus; KFC, Yao (Chinese), Juici Patties, Burger King, Welfare (Jamaican), and The Spot (Jamaican); the ‘big six’. We are now delivering from additional restaurants to provide even a wider variety to our customers.

If you are looking for your food to be delivered faster, and at a better price too, then Big 6 Delivery is for you. We are a happy company, our customers are important to us, we keep them happy, so too our employees. We are a family at Big 6 Delivery; customers included. We hope that you’ll try our services and stick around to become a #HappyBig6ixer too.
